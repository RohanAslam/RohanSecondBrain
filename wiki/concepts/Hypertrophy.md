# Hypertrophy

The physiological process of muscle growth through an increase in the size of muscle fibers. The primary goal of resistance training for most recreational lifters.

---

## Key Drivers

- **[[Training Volume]]** — total sets per muscle per week is the most studied variable
- **[[Progressive Overload]]** — must increase stimulus over time to continue growing
- **Recovery** — muscle is built between sessions, not during them
- **Protein synthesis** — adequate dietary protein is required for adaptation
